About

Founded in 1996, Għaqda Armar Għajnsielem's main mission is to enrich our community and celebrate our traditions. Our core goals are:

Feast Decorations: We take pride in decorating the village for our annual titular feast, making it a vibrant and beautiful celebration for all.

Village Events: We also work to decorate Għajnsielem for other special events throughout the year, adding a touch of festivity to community life.

Community Involvement: We are committed to helping young people and other residents get involved in social activities and showcase their talents. We believe in the power of community and the importance of working together.

We're always looking for new members and volunteers! If you'd like to contribute your skills or simply get involved, please don't hesitate to reach out.

TimeLine

Għaqda Armar Għajnsielem over the years.

  • 1996

    The Għaqda Armar Għajnsielem organization was founded and the old storage facility was built.

  • 2000-2003

    The square's "brazzi" (decorative arms for the lampposts) were made.

  • 2000-2004

    Six statues for the main square were commissioned.

  • 2004

    The organization began replacing the decorative banners on Mgarr Road.

  • 2004

    Eight decorative arms for the main square's circumference were made, based on a design by Rudolph.

  • 2005

    The arch was brought out for the first time.

  • 2006

    The second storage facility was opened.

  • 2013

    The organization was registered as a Voluntary Organization (VO).

  • 2015

    The "Madonna tad-dimostrazzjoni" (Madonna of the demonstration) statue was completed by Adonai Camilleri Cauchi.

  • 2023

    A new set of statues for the old square was completed.

  • 2023-2024

    Replacing the decorative light banners on Ghajnsielem Street.

  • 2025

    New decorations for old storage facility's facade were made located in the back of the suqar.

  • 2025

    Work began on replacing the decorative light banners on Simirat Street.
